• Emoji search improvements make it easier to find the right way to say I love you using whatever heart-shaped emoji best matches the state of your own heart.
• Photos in the attachment selector are now sorted based on how recently they were added to your device. Go ahead and download an old meme and share it with a new friend. Even if the picture was originally created several years ago, you won't have to scroll forever to spot it in the list anymore.
