Não reunimos os seus dados para os vender e não lucramos com anúncios, portanto contamos com as suas doações para manter o Signal em funcionamento:
• You can now help sustain Signal by contributing a small donation each month using Apple Pay. Sustainers earn fun badges they can display on their profile to help spread awareness. 
• Give Signal a boost using Apple Pay. Boosts are a quick one-time donation that let’s you say thanks and keep Signal going. Boosts come with their own unique badge.

This release also includes numerous bug fixes.
